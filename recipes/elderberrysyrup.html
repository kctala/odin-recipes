Elderberry Syrup

Description

This is Anna and Joey Albright's recipe for Elderberry Syrup that was passed down from Joey's family. It tastes so yummy!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried elderberries
  • 4 cups of water (some recipes use only 2 cups)
  • 2-4 cinnamon sticks
  • several pieces of fresh ginger
  • 1 cup of honey
  • Alternatively, if using fresh berries it would be 1 cup of berries with 1 cup of water and the same spices

Instructions

  1. Add elderberries, water, cinnamon, and ginger to a pot and simmer for about 45 minutes.
  2. Make sure the ingredients have reduced by about 50%.
  3. Cool to 118 deg F.
  4. Add 1 cup of honey.
  5. Stir well and place syrup in the fridge.
